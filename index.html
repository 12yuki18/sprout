SPROUTING PROJECT

BILLIONS OF PAPER (PLASTIC LINED) COFFEE CUPS MAKE THEIR WAY TO LAND FILL EVERY YEAR. OUR COMPOSTABLE CUPS ARE MADE FROM PLANT PULP AND ARE 100% BIODEGRADABLE WHEN DISPOSED OF CORRECTLY.
